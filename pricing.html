The BooksToday Standard

Included With Every Plan

We don't nickel and dime you. These essential tools and protections come standard regardless of which pricing tier you choose.

Strict NDA Protection

Your ideas are your own. The moment you upload a manuscript to our portal, it is automatically protected by a legally binding Non-Disclosure Agreement. Your intellectual property is completely secure with us.

Dedicated Dashboard

No more losing drafts in long email chains. You get a secure, personalized dashboard where you can sign contracts, upload new chapters, and track your editing progress in real-time.

Direct Editor Messaging

Communication is key to a good edit. You will have direct access to message your assigned editor through our platform to ask questions, clarify notes, or brainstorm changes.

Track Changes Formatting

Every edit is presented as a suggestion. We return your document heavily annotated using Microsoft Word's Track Changes, meaning you have the final authority to accept or reject any edit we make.

Our Ironclad Guarantee

If you are not completely satisfied with your sample edit, you owe us nothing. We stand by the quality of our work.
