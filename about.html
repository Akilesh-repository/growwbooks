Our Journey

The History of BooksToday

2016

The Inception

BooksToday was founded by a small group of three passionate freelance editors sitting in a coffee shop, realizing independent authors needed better access to traditional publishing-level critiques.

2018

Digital Expansion

We launched our first online portal, allowing authors globally to submit their drafts securely. This year, we successfully edited and polished over 150 full-length manuscripts.

2021

Genre Specialists

To ensure high-quality feedback, we expanded our team to include dedicated genre specialists. From Sci-Fi world-builders to Non-Fiction fact-checkers, our roster grew to 25 elite editors.

What We Stand For

Our Core Editorial Values

Preserve the Voice

An editor's job is not to rewrite the book in their own style. We meticulously ensure that your unique authorial voice remains intact and shines brighter through our edits.

Strict Confidentiality

Your intellectual property is fiercely protected. We operate under strict NDAs, ensuring your unreleased manuscripts, plot ideas, and outlines are completely secure.

Constructive Honesty

We don't sugarcoat structural issues, but we never tear down an author. Our feedback is always detailed, highly actionable, and delivered with absolute professionalism.

Punctuality

Publishing runs on tight schedules. When we agree on a deadline for your manuscript return, we hit it. Period. You will always know exactly when to expect your edits back.

Genre Fluency

A romance novel cannot be edited the same way as a dry technical manual. We respect genre conventions, reader expectations, and the specific pacing required.

No AI Replacements

We never use Generative AI to rewrite your prose. Your book is evaluated by a real human being with a pulse, an education, and a deep love for literature.
