Our Methodical Process

We maintain strict transparency. Track your manuscript's progress chapter-by-chapter in your client dashboard.

1

Submission & Quote

Submit a sample chapter via our secure portal. We will assess the manuscript's current state, match you with the ideal editor, and provide a transparent, flat-rate quote and timeline.

2

The First Pass

Your assigned editor performs the heavy lifting. Depending on the service level, this involves structural critique, line-by-line track changes, and detailed margin notes explaining editorial choices.

3

Author Review & Revisions

We return the marked-up manuscript. You review the suggestions, accept or reject changes, and implement structural rewrites. You can message your editor directly through the dashboard.

4

Final Polish

Once your revisions are complete, we do a final, meticulous sweep to catch any lingering typos, formatting errors, or inconsistencies before you hit publish.
